Features

  • Secured Email Access: Fetches emails from the user‚Äôs Gmail inbox, allowing users to access and manage their emails within the app.
  • Unread Email Summaries: Summarizes unread emails and provides a digest of important information.
  • Conversational Interface: Provides an engaging, interactive experience while managing emails.
  • AI-Powered Summaries: Delivers conversational, AI-generated email summaries to help users quickly understand their inboxes.
  • Voice Integration: Allows users to manage their emails through voice commands, promoting inclusivity for users with physical disabilities.
  • Daily Email Summary: Offers a daily digest of emails, tailored to the user‚Äôs preferences.
  • Actionable Recommendations: Generates actionable items from email content, making it easy to respond or take action directly from the app.

Technologies Used

  • Kotlin: Programming language used for Android development.
  • Jetpack Compose: UI toolkit for building native Android UIs.
  • Firebase: Authentication and backend services.
  • Hugging Face API: For generating email summaries and recommendations using the Mistralai/Mistral-Nemo-Instruct-2407 model.
